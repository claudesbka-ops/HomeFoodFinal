FAQ

How do I track my order?

You can track your order in the app under "My Orders" or click the tracking link in your order confirmation email.

What if I need to cancel my order?

Orders can be cancelled within 30 minutes of placing them. After that, cancellation depends on the home cook's preparation status.

How do I become a home cook?

Download the app and apply through the "Become a Cook" section. You'll need to complete verification and meet our kitchen standards.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, online banking, and e-wallets (Touch 'n Go, GrabPay).

How do refunds work?

Refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days after approval.
