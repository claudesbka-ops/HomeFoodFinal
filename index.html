FAQ

Questions about earning.

Can't find your answer? Email support@homefood.my

How much can I earn as a home cook on HomeFood?

Most active cooks earn between RM2,000 and RM8,000 per month. Top performers cooking full-time can exceed RM10,000. You keep 70% of every order. Earnings depend on hours, ratings, and dish variety.

Which cities can I cook in?

We're currently active in Kuala Lumpur and Selangor. Penang and Johor Bahru are launching in Q3 2026 — sign up now to be notified when your area opens.

Do I need cooking experience or a food business license?

No experience or license is required to start. We provide free guidance on food safety, hygiene, and packaging. Optional certifications are available and can boost your visibility and earnings.

What are the requirements to become a home cook?

You need a Malaysian IC, a clean home kitchen, a smartphone, and a bank account for payouts. That's it — sign up takes about 2 minutes.

How do I get paid?

Earnings are deposited daily into your registered Malaysian bank account. No waiting periods. No hidden fees. You can also withdraw on demand from the app.

Can I work part-time or full-time?

Both. You set your own hours and toggle availability anytime. Many of our cooks are parents and students who cook a few hours a day.

What if I can't fulfill an order?

Simply decline incoming orders that don't fit your capacity. You can also pause new orders any time from the app — no penalty.

How do I get more orders and higher earnings?

Maintain a 4.8+ rating, post clear dish photos, respond quickly to orders, and keep a varied menu. Top performers also unlock 5% extra commission.
